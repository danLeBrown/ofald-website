## About This Event

The **Olubukola Foundation for Autism and Learning Disability (OFALD)** presents this online webinar to address the critical challenges faced by individuals with neurodevelopmental conditions in Nigeria. The session, titled **"Breaking Barriers and Building Inclusion,"** aims to provide practical insights and strategies for creating a more supportive society.

## Speakers & Moderators

We are honored to host a panel of experienced professionals:

* **Speaker:** Mrs. Adewumi Adeyemi
* **Speaker:** Dr. Oluwatosin Ayantoyinbo
* **Moderator:** Mrs. Gloria Okpanachi

## What to Expect

* Expert-led discussions on understanding autism and various learning disabilities
* Strategies for breaking societal barriers and promoting inclusion within the Nigerian context
* Interactive Q&A session with healthcare and education specialists
* Guidance on resources available for families and caregivers

## Who Should Attend

* Parents and caregivers of children with special needs
* Healthcare professionals and therapists
* Educators, school owners, and administrators
* Advocates for social inclusion and disability rights
