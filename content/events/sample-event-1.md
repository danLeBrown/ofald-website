## About This Event

This community awareness campaign aims to educate the public about autism and learning disabilities, reduce stigma, and promote understanding and inclusion in our communities.

## What to Expect

- Information booths with resources about autism and learning disabilities
- Expert talks from healthcare professionals
- Interactive sessions for families
- Networking opportunities with other families and supporters

## Who Should Attend

- Families with children with autism or learning disabilities
- Community members interested in learning more
- Healthcare professionals
- Educators and school administrators
