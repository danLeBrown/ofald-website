## About This Outreach

OFALD, in partnership with doctors, speech and language therapists, psychologists, and physiotherapists from Nigeria and around the world, offers free consultations and bi-annual health checks for people with autism and learning disabilities.

## Services Available

- General health consultations
- Speech and language therapy assessments
- Psychological evaluations
- Physiotherapy consultations
- Mental health support for families

## Locations

- **Lagos**: Main OFALD Office
- **Ibadan**: OFALD Training Center
- **Osogbo**: Community Health Center
- **Abuja**: Partnership Facility
