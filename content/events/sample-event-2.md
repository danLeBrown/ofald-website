## About This Workshop

This workshop is designed to teach practical skills in pottery and crafts to young people with learning disabilities aged 14-21. Participants will learn basic techniques and create their own pieces to take home.

## Workshop Details

- **Duration**: Full day (9:00 AM - 3:00 PM)
- **Cost**: Free
- **Materials**: All materials provided
- **Age Group**: 14-21 years old

## What You'll Learn

- Basic pottery techniques
- Hand-building methods
- Craft skills and creativity
- Finishing and glazing basics
